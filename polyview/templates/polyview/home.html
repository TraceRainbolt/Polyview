Polyview

What can it do?

Polyview is a very powerful website. Here are a couple of the features it supports:

  • Search for classes by Polyrating.
  • Get instant e-mails when courses add sections.
  • Advanced scheduling options.
  • And much more!

How does it work?

Using polyview is simple. Follow these steps to get started:

  • Sign-In if you want to save your courses.
  • Find the courses you want.
  • Search through the possible schedules.
  • Access your saved courses through your account.
